B.Sc. Computer Science
B.Sc. (Hons.) Computer Science
Five-year honours programme covering fundamentals of computing, programming, and applied technology.
UG-PG integrated
5 Years (10 Semesters) 25 Seats CUET (UG) 10+2 with Mathematics

The B.Sc. (Hons.) Computer Science programme provides a rigorous foundation in programming languages, data structures, algorithms, operating systems, database management, and computer networks. Students are introduced to modern fields like web development, mobile computing, and introductory artificial intelligence. The programme follows NEP 2020 with multiple exit options and credit-based flexibility.

Core subjects: C/C++, Java, Data Structures, DBMS, OS, Networks, Discrete Mathematics

M.Sc. Computer Science
M.Sc. Computer Science
Advanced postgraduate programme for deep specialisation in core and applied computer science.
Postgraduate
2 Years (4 Semesters) 40 Seats CUET (PG) B.Sc. CS / BCA / B.Tech.

The M.Sc. Computer Science programme at CUSB is designed for graduates who wish to deepen their understanding of advanced algorithms, computer architecture, distributed systems, machine learning, and research methodologies. The programme emphasizes independent research culminating in a dissertation project in the final semester, equipping students for careers in research, academia, and industry.

Advanced Algorithms · Distributed Systems · ML · Cloud Computing · Research Dissertation

M.Sc. Artificial Intelligence
M.Sc. Artificial Intelligence
India's growing AI ecosystem needs specialised talent. CUSB's M.Sc. AI programme delivers exactly that.
M.Sc. AI
2 Years (4 Semesters) 20 Seats CUET (PG) >B.Sc. CS / BCA / B.Tech. (CS/IT)

The M.Sc. Artificial Intelligence programme at CUSB is a cutting-edge postgraduate degree designed to equip students with the theoretical foundations and practical skills needed to build, deploy, and evaluate intelligent systems. Covering deep learning, natural language processing, computer vision, reinforcement learning, and AI ethics — this programme prepares students for careers in AI research, product companies, and national AI missions.

Deep Learning · NLP · Computer Vision · RL · PyTorch · TensorFlow · AI Ethics · Capstone Project

Doctoral Research
Ph.D. Programme
PhD — Computer Science
Full-time and part-time doctoral research across all areas of computer science and AI.
Ph.D.
3 – 5 Years As per Vacancies Interview + Research Proposal M.Sc. / M.Tech. (55%+)

The Ph.D. programme in Computer Science at CUSB offers full-time and part-time doctoral research under the guidance of experienced faculty. Research areas include artificial intelligence, machine learning, computer vision, natural language processing, network security, distributed computing, bioinformatics, and computational theory. Scholars receive CUET-NET/JRF fellowships as applicable.

Research Areas: AI · ML · Security · Distributed Systems · Bioinformatics · Theory of Computation
