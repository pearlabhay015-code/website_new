Girls Hostel — Gargi Sadan & Maitreyi Sadan

Gargi Sadan

Maitreyi Sadan

Girls Hostel

The hostel administration takes all care to provide quality accommodation to the students so that they may feel to be at home. The rooms are well-furnished with basic amenities for a comfortable living. There are separate blocks for the girls and boys with all essential facilities. The mess building is well organised with advanced kitchen equipment to provide quality services to the inmates. The hostel is also having ample open space with beautiful garden in its premises. The key facilities are as following:

-- Infrastructure and Amenities : --

•Fully furnished rooms (on sharing basis) equipped with wooden bed, wardrobe and a study table with chair
